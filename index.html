Operations Analytics & Predictive Modeling
Predictive Staffing Optimization: Apple Genius Bar

Designed a predictive staffing model for Apple's Genius Bar to replace static weekly scheduling with demand-driven staffing across 530+ stores. The model integrates weather forecasts, product launch calendars, and appointment booking patterns to predict hourly walk-in traffic 7 days out. Uses walk-forward time-series cross-validation with MAE/MAPE metrics. Nightly batch predictions feed into workforce management via API, shifting operations from intuition-based templates to dynamic, data-informed shift generation.

Predictive ModelingDemand ForecastingTime-Series AnalysisStaffing OptimizationOperations DesignChange Management
Why This Matters

Transforms scheduling from repeated templates and manager intuition into a model-driven system. The before/after framework shows a clear operational shift: weather, launch events, and booking data feed hourly predictions that auto-generate optimized shifts with a continuous feedback loop.
